Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Study Stack ("we", "us", the "Service") collects, uses, and protects your information. By creating an account or using the Service, you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Account data: your email address and an encrypted password, handled by Google Firebase Authentication.
  • App data you create: your tasks, weekly routines, study-timer history, "Lock In" sessions, and Motivation settings (rival name and quotes). This is stored in Google Firestore under your account ID.
  • Payment references: if you use the "Lock In" give-up option, we store the bKash Transaction ID (TrxID) and amount you submit, so payments can be verified. We do not collect or store your bKash PIN, OTP, or account credentials.
  • Technical data: standard log/diagnostic data that hosting and Firebase services generate automatically.

2. How we use your information

  • To provide and sync your study data across your devices.
  • To operate the "Lock In" feature, including verifying give-up payments.
  • To secure accounts and prevent abuse.
  • To contact you about your account (e.g. password resets, important changes).

3. Payments

Study Stack is free to use. The only charge is the optional "give up" fee in Lock In mode, paid by you directly through bKash to the number shown in the app. We receive the Transaction ID you submit; we do not process card details or handle your bKash login. Give-up fees are non-refundable (see the Terms of Service).

4. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with infrastructure providers that run the Service — primarily Google Firebase (authentication, database, hosting). Your data may be processed on their servers outside Bangladesh.

5. Data retention & your rights

You can, from inside the app's ⚙ Account & Privacy menu:

  • Export all of your data as a JSON file.
  • Delete your account, which permanently removes your stored data.

You may also email us to request access, correction, or deletion of your data.

6. Children

The Service is intended for students aged 13 and above. If you are under the age required by local law, use the Service only with a parent or guardian's consent.

7. Security

We use Firebase Authentication and per-user database security rules so that each user can access only their own data. No online service can guarantee absolute security, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
